<p>
	An invisible cone of terror causes each living creature in the area to become <a href="/srd/conditionSummary.htm#panicked">panicked</a> unless it succeeds on a <a href="/srd/combat/combatStatistics.htm#will">Will save</a>. If cornered, a panicked creature begins <a href="/srd/conditionSummary.htm#cowering">cowering</a>. If the Will save succeeds, the creature is <a href="/srd/conditionSummary.htm#shaken">shaken</a> for 1 round.
</p>

<h6>Material Component</h6>
<p>
	Either the heart of a hen or a white feather.
